August 14, 2025 - Public Hearing Date Set

The Ada County Planning and Zoning Commission has scheduled a public hearing for the shooting range conditional use permit application. The hearing will take place on August 14th, 2025 at 6:00 PM.

Key Takeaways:

  • Public hearing on August 14th, 2025 at 6:00 PM
  • Location: 200 W Front Street, Boise (Room #1235) (map)
  • Can attend in-person or via WebEx
  • WebEx testimony requires sign-up by August 11th
  • This is the community's opportunity to voice concerns

To Testify via WebEx:
You must sign up by close of business the Monday before the public hearing (August 11, 2025).
Sign up here: https://adacounty.id.gov/developmentservices/public-hearing-testimony-sign-up/

Your voice matters!
 
If you can make it to the meeting to speak in person, your impact will be amplified. We urge you to show up and speak. If you can't get to Boise but want to attend (in person or online) and need help - reach out.

June 26, 2025 - Shooting range seeks to become permanent

According to BoiseDev, The landowner submitted a conditional use permit application but is doubling down on a defiant strategy

Key Takeaways:

  • Submitted application but didn't pay fees
  • Asserts ability for paying customers to shoot "any hours of any day"
  • Doesn't believe they need a permit
  • Opens the door to other commercial activity for ‘All invited guests of whatever nature and kind’

Source: BoiseDev
